About me

I am a software engineer / researcher at LINE Corporation. My research interests include statistical speech synthesis, voice conversion, and machine learning. Before joining LINE Corporation, I worked in music signal processsing, music information retrieval, and computer vision.

I love to write code and enjoy open-source collaboration on GitHub.

I am also a doctoral student at Toda Laboratory in Nagoya University.
