About me

I am a software engineer / researcher at LINE Corporation. My research interests include statistical speech synthesis, voice conversion, and machine learning. Prior to joining LINE Corporation, I worked for music signal processsing, music information retrieval, and computer vision.

In my spare time, I love to write code and enjoy open-source collaboration on Github.
